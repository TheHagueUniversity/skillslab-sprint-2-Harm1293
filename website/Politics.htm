Politics

Politics is one of the most difficult subjects to discuss, we tend to not be very open minded regarding the information we receive about political leaders from other countries besides our own. We tend to categorize political views different than democratic as wrong. This leads to a biasedly formed opinion regarding world events which makes us easier to manipulate. The purpose of this page is to inform as many sides of the argument as possible in an objective way. It is important to know and understand both sides of a conflict specially during these times of instability in the international relations between mayor geopolitical players.
